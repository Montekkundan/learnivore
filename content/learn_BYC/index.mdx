# Welcome to Before You Code!

Before You Code is your comprehensive pre-coding handbook, designed to guide you through the labyrinth of programming languages, frameworks, and libraries. Each episode delves into the nitty-gritty of a particular tool, providing a thorough examination before you decide to invest your time and effort in learning it.

## What's Inside Before You Code?

- **In-depth Reviews:** Get a well-rounded understanding of different programming tools, their strengths, weaknesses, and unique features.
- **Installation Guides:** Step-by-step instructions to get you up and running with the tools discussed.
- **Community and Support:** Explore the support and resources available to help you on your learning journey.
- **Real-world Applications:** Discover how these tools are applied in real-world scenarios and what you can build with them.
- ... and much more!

In addition to the written insights, each Before You Code episode comes with a corresponding YouTube tutorial. The video content is crafted to enhance your understanding, providing a visual walkthrough of the concepts discussed in the document.

## Have Questions?

Embark on an informed coding journey with Before You Code, and ensure you're making the right choices in your learning endeavors!
